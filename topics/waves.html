üìà What is a Wave?

A wave is a disturbance that transfers energy through space or matter without transferring the matter itself. Waves carry energy from one place to another while the medium (if present) oscillates in place.

Key Concept: When you drop a stone in water, the ripples carry energy outward, but the water molecules mainly move up and down, not outward with the wave. The wave transfers energy, not matter.
